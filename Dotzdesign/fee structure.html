The following fee structure is for clients of home staging of residential and commercial property.

Initial Briefing: Assessing the space, finding out your requirements, your likes/ dislikes, possible budgets. This will be charged at an hourly rate.

Conceptual Presentation: After initial briefing a conceptual presentation will be created in accordance with your requirements, ie: digital mood board, floorplan or 3D design. This will be charged at an hourly rate.

This would include a follow up meeting to discuss the presentation and finalize any changes that are to be made. 		  If you are only wanting the concept for inspiration and wish to complete the work yourself, we offer a mood board and advice..

However, if you are wanting Dotzdesign to execute the concept to final stage, we work on a commission base on top of the project costing.

Expanding on the above, we also offer the following services:

Renovations: If you are undertaking a renovation and not just looking to decorate, we can assist you. Dotzdesign can conceptualize the revised interior and offer building plans with service layout. We can also obtain plan approval on Municipal submissions, if required. 		  Project Managment: After finalizing business plans, we manage construction to final stage. However, this is not only for layouts we designed, for a fee, we can manage all projects.

Space Planning: We can also offer space planning for residential or commercial properties. This would include working with what you have and recognizing it to its fullest advantage. This can be achieved through physical implementation or layout.
